About Intentify

Intentify is a lightweight browser-based message analysis tool for testing, validation, and early launch. It estimates intent using language patterns, not psychology or certainty.

Privacy

Your text stays in your browser. The app does not require login and does not send your message to a paid model or external API.

Disclaimer

Intentify provides interpretation based on wording patterns. It is not legal, psychological, or professional advice.

How accurate is this?

It is a rule-based estimator. It works best for common language patterns like avoidance, receptiveness, soft rejection, and overly defensive phrasing. It should be treated as guidance, not certainty.

Does it use AI or store user data?

No external AI is required in this version. Analysis runs locally in the browser, and saved state is limited to your own device via localStorage.

Can this be monetized later?

Yes. The clean path is to launch this free browser-first version, validate demand, then add a paid AI mode, screenshot analysis, conversation threads, or premium templates.
